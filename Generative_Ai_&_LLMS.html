Generative Ai & LLMs

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can create new content, such as text, images, or music, based on patterns and examples learned from data. They often utilize techniques like neural networks to generate realistic outputs. Large Language Models (LLMs) are a type of generative AI specifically designed for text generation tasks. They are trained on vast amounts of text data and can generate coherent and contextually relevant text given a prompt or a cue. Examples include GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) models like GPT-3.5, capable of generating human-like text across various domains and topics.
